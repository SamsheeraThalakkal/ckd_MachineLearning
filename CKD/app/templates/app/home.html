Early Detection of Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a progressive condition that affects the kidneys’ ability to filter waste from the blood. Early detection plays a critical role in slowing down disease progression, improving quality of life, and preventing kidney failure.

Many CKD symptoms appear only in later stages. With the help of modern predictive models, such as Random Forest and LightGBM, we can identify potential CKD cases much earlier. These AI-driven models analyze medical indicators like blood pressure, serum creatinine, hemoglobin levels, and more — helping doctors and patients take timely action.
